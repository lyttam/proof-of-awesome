Raised in an Iowa farming community, I emigrated to Madison, Wisconsin after receiving my 4-year degree from a liberal arts college. When not writing code, I can be found loving life in appreciation of one or more of my favorite things: food, exercise, and the great outdoors. An ideal weekend for me is one where I load my bicycle up with camping gear and head into the wilderness to cook over a fire. I also enjoy gardening, trying to avoid sunburn, and whiskey.

Colleagues and friends know me for my dry humor and my cooperative nature. On teams of people I often find myself in a “mediator/communicator” role, understanding the different sides of situations and helping those sides understand each other. I’m a rational thinker and independent person who thrives on making valuable contributions to something larger than myself.
